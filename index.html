            I greatly enjoyed the newest addition to the ever expanding Final Fantasy series. Not as heart-wrenching as VI nor memorable as VII. Without a doubt worth any long-time fan or new gamers time to play through the storyline. The game has its flaws that can drag down the experience with repetitive actions. Therefore, this guide focuses on three aspects of the game that will greatly aid in cutting down repetitive actions to allocate more time to enjoying the storyline and epic battles against monsters that will test your sanity.

            Three aspects of the game that are essential are: gil (money), AP and fishing. With a MASSIVE world to explore will make gaining these items daunting. This guide will focus upon the fastest ways to gain these items. Also, helpful tips on how I recommand using these items.
