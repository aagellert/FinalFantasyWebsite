    Sea access is only available in Royal Edition. Currently, there are only three ports in which you can launch from with the Royal Vessel. The fishing spots are scattered across the sea. Each time you enter into the sea the fishing spots will route. The routing fishing spots is to encourage users to explore instead of fishing in the same place each time.


    Altissia

    Main Continent




    I spent countless hours trying to find fish and even more so to find the rare gems. This is due to the fact that there is no real tutorial to teach how to find fishing spots. To save you time and your sanity, look for the red spots. Majority of the fishing areas will be green spots. These areas are good for users with fishing level less than 8 or who have not completed the fishing quest (the fishing quest can be found under Land Fishing). If you have maxed the fishing level and finished the fishing quest, you want to find the red spots and Bismarck. Bismark can only be found in the sea after activating the quest 'A Second Sea God' in Altissia (quest starts in the restaurant.) Also, while fishing, remember if you don't need gil, save the items for crafting magic (video for crafting magic can be found in under Land Fishing).
