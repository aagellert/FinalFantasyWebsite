    Main Continents

    Altissia



    The best starting point is Vannath Coast (location 10). This area has the lowest level of fish and cheap material to get started. With a shop right next to the fishing spot allows for quick selling of material (game only allows a maximum of 99 fish in inventory). While fishing here, it would be best to get to fishing level 4-5 before moving to Rachsia Bridge. The best fish to get at a low level is the Nebula Salmon. Both the scales and meat sells well.

    Most important! fish fillet is one of the best material to craft magic. Fish fillet gives magical items healing for the caster and massive boost to attack. When not fishing for extra gil, use the fillet for magic crafting. Please watch the video on how to craft magic by LemonEatingKow (2017) from YouTube.




    A great quest to participate in is the quest offered by the NPC Navyth. He can first be located by the southern shore of the Alstor Slough lake (location 14). The first mission is Fishing Buddies (starts after beginning Chapter 3). These quests will provide free gil, exp and upgraded fishing items.


    Altissia, City on the Sea, is not accessible until after Chapter 9. This tiny city on the sea has a few fishing spots. The only advantage that Altissia has compared to the mainland is having easy access to higher level fish that are found only in the sea (fished from the docks).
