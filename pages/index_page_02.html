        Fan Theories

            Final Fantasy series is surrounded by fan theories built upon tidbits of information provided by the creators. In some cases, the theories are proven correct when the creators release additional content (expansions, mangas...etc). In the case of FFXV, there is a lot of information to gleam through.

            However, none of these theories will make sense unless you actually play the game. I highly suggest playing the game first and returning to this page to delve into theories that I believe have the best evidence to be true.


            Ardyn is a tragic character. A King of Eos and gifted with powers to save his people. To save the people, he must absorb the darkness that afflicts them within himself. The darkness he consumed beings to deteriorate both his mind and soul. A theory is that the one who pushes Ardyn over the edge into maddess is his brother, Somnus the Mystic. Somnus is remembered as a great King but there is no mention of Ardyn being his brother. The only reason we are aware that Ardyn is related to Somuns and a King, is due to Ardyn's own confessions. A man who manipulates others should never be trusted. At the end of the game just before facing Ardyn, Somuns requests the protagonist to save his brother. Also, Ardyn states that Somuns erased him from history just before passing away.
