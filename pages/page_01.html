    Each character within the game has a special survival skill. The protagonist, Noctis, has the skill of fishing. In early game, fishing is a useful skill for it can provide: gil, AP and additional cooking items. In this guide, I will teach you the basic of fishing and areas to fish.


    Controls for fishing differs depending upon the device (pc/xbox/ps4). The basic concept is exactly the same regardless of the controls. The video below is an introduction to fishing. The original video is created by Jay Cartere (2016) from YouTube.




    Before chapter 10 the only available places to fish is on land. There are fishing spots scattered throughout the world. Regardless of level, the fishing areas area easily accessible and no enemies can enter into the fishing areas. After chapter 10 and only if you have purchased the Royal Edition Expansion (or the FF15 Royal Edition version) open sea fishing will be available. I have included a guide to help with decided which places are the best to explore.
