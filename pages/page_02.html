    • AP Guide


    AP is essential. Gaining levels will aid in increasing characters strengths. Additonal abilities such as extra armor or strengthening of magic is based upon the ascension points that have to be unlocked. Each point is unlocked with AP. AP is extremely hard to gain for each battle (not consistently) will earn 1 AP. Fish only earn 1 AP till upgraded in the ascension grid (which costs 555 AP). Grinding to get AP is extremely tedious unless you know a secret. The best places to gain fast AP is in Three Valleys. I have circled on the map which area you want to visit.



    The first ascension that you wish to unlock is 'Warp Strike'. This ability will give you 1 AP for each enemy defeated by warping. When unlocked, defeat the monsters Sabertusk (which are found close to the skeleton) using warp. Since, they are low level, with low defense, the monsters will be defeated within seconds. After all monsters are defeated, summon them again using the beast whistle. When available do the time quest located within the Three Valley. For it will spawn 50 Sabertusk and is repeatable till the next IRL day.
