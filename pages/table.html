    Earning money early game is a discouraging task with little gil given at the start of the game. An easy task that can be used endlessly after Iris moves to Cape Caem is farming carrots.

    Even though the game limits planting of carrots to 4. Carrots can be planted each in game day or after quests. The fastest way to plant carrots is by planting them and sleeping in the farm house to reset the day to plant more.


    Carrots can be traded for gil or other expensive items. I have created a table for all tradable items (some items are random).

    Name Type Carrot Price
    Force Steeler Upgradable Weapon 4
    Plunderers Upgradable Weapon 4
    Valiant Upgradable Weapon 4
    Stickers Decor for Regalia 3
    500 gil Money 1
